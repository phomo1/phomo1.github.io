About us

The Philosophy of Memory Organization (PhOMO) is an international network that aims to foster interaction among philosophers interested in the study of memory and its relationship to other cognitive phenomena and philosophical issues.

PhOMO was founded at the Issues in Philosophy of Memory conference held in 2017 in Cologne. The last iteration of the conference, Issues in Philosophy of Memory 2, was held in 2019 in Grenoble.
